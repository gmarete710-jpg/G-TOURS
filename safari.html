Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit Kenya?

The dry seasons (June-October and January-February) are ideal for wildlife viewing. However, Kenya is beautiful year-round. The Great Migration is best viewed July-August.

Do I need any vaccinations?

It's recommended to consult your doctor, but common vaccines include Yellow Fever, Malaria prevention, and routine vaccinations.

What wildlife will I see?

Kenya is home to the "Big Five" (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, rhino) plus zebras, giraffes, hyenas, antelopes, and over 600 bird species.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Yes! We have family-friendly packages with kid-appropriate activities and flexible schedules. Children find safaris both educational and exciting.

What's included in the packages?

Packages typically include accommodation, meals, transportation, and guided tours. Specific inclusions vary - check individual package details.

Can I customize my safari?

Absolutely! Contact us to create a personalized safari experience tailored to your interests, budget, and preferences.
