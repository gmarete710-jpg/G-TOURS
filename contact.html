Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to contact you?

You can reach us via email (garethmarete11@gmail.com), phone (+254 700 123 456), or by filling out the contact form above. Response time is typically within 24 hours.

How far in advance should I book a tour?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance for better availability and planning. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and PayPal. A 30% deposit is required to confirm booking, with the balance due 30 days before travel.

Do you offer customized tours?

Yes! We specialize in creating custom tour packages tailored to your specific interests, budget, and schedule. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 45 days before travel receive a full refund. 15-45 days gets 50% refund. Less than 15 days is non-refundable. Travel insurance is recommended.

Do you provide travel insurance?

While we don't provide insurance directly, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance before your trip. Ask us for recommended providers.
